A Bit About Me

Hello all, I am Emily McCosh, writer of strange things and daydreamer extraordinaire. I blog occasionally and upload writing- and book-related videos on youtube often. Basically once a week. Seriously, it’s getting goofier as we speak. I also design things, because just writing isn’t enough, I guess.

I own two monster dogs that talk even more than I do, am a student at Cal Poly San Luis Obispo, and have been published at Beneath Ceaseless Skies, Shimmer, Galaxy’s Edge, Flash Fiction Online, and eslewhere.

Currently, there are approximately 3,042 ideas knocking around the inside of my skull. Send help.
